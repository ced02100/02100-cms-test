Hardware product Website & Shop

Recovery is what allows us to cross boundaries, to rise above ourselves and become better people.  That's why Hydragun wants to change the way our body recovers - so athletes can achieve more. This is how the Hydragun was born: The next generation regeneration solution for bodybuilders, runners, martial artists and all those who want to take their sport to the next level.

For the Singapore based company around Aloysius Chay we implemented the complete web presence with Webflow and some custom code integrations. In the following you can find the summarized highlights.

1. Custom Testimonial Slider

In order to visualize the positive customer feedback of Hydragun appropriately, we have implemented a custom slider. This has the special functionality that several images but only one review is displayed at a time and that reviews can be browsed endlessly by drag-and-drop. The slick slider was integrated with custom code, because this requirement could not be realized with Webflow alone.

2. 3D Product Lottie Animation

Video and image material is the most important form of visualization for hardware products. In the best case, a prospective buyer only sees the image/video material and immediately feels the desire to buy. Therefore we have displayed the Hydragun as many times as possible. In a technically sophisticated way, a 360° video of the product was transformed into an on-scroll lottie animation that shows the product from all sides.

3. Shopify Integration

Besides the landing page, the Hydragun shop page has some special features. Basically the whole page is designed with Webflow. Only the checkout process, the product slider and the reviews are accessed live from Shopify. So the site benefits from the functionality of Shopify and the design possibilities of Webflow.
Here you can check out the live website of Hydragun.

Lead-Gen Website & dynamic CMS Blog

Univatio helps students to find answers to their questions. With educational posts on various topics such as university, taxes and retirement provisions, students can train themselves and, if desired, apply for advice from Univatio's professional portfolio of consultants.

For the German company around Ivan Grischanov, we designed the corporate identity, created wireframes and implemented them in Webflow. Finally, the site was optimized for SEO. In the following the summarized highlights.

1. CI Design with Adobe XD

In several feedback loops, style guides were first created that gave Univatio a feeling for your corporate identity. After deciding on a design, the landing page was first created in an Adobe XD wireframe and discussed again. When everyone was satisfied, the landing page was implemented in Webflow and the remaining pages were implemented according to the design of the landing page.

2. SEO Optimization

Especially in the lead-gen area, SEO optimization is essential for the business model to work. Accordingly, we have always followed best practices for performance, optimized the headings H1-H4 and set up Open Graph via Webflow for Univatio.

3. Dynamic CMS Blog

Part of the marketing strategy of Univatio is content creation. The company aims to generate coverage through articles that provide added value for students. With Webflow CMS we have implemented a blog structure for Univatio, which is very easy to maintain and expand. All placeholders can now be filled in the Webflow Editor. The rest happens automatically. For example, the reading suggestions are automatically updated at the end of each post. This makes it very easy to add new blog posts. Technically interesting is the automatically updating table of contents, which we added with Custom Code.
Here you can find the live website of Univatio.

Website for SaaS Startup

Intrro uses data to automatically suggest promising candidates in employee networks as recommendations for open positions in companies.

For the London and Dubai based company we implemented wireframes in Webflow. The focus was especially on the animations of the landing page. Overall, intrro.com is a very lean and minimalistic startup website. In the following the summarized highlights.

1. Landing Page Animationen, that make the Website feel alive

Since Intrro is software designed to simplify interpersonal interaction, it was important to the founders from the very beginning that the website feels as alive as possible. Besides floating (flogo) animations and slide-in effects, there are some on-scroll animations.

2. Click to Scroll CTA

To animate the visitor to read on, a constantly rotating call-to-action in the Hero section was created with custom code. With a click the website scrolls independently to the next (content) section.

3. On-Scroll Timeline

Another interesting animation at Intrro is the "How it works" process, which we illustrated with a timeline. While the visitor scrolls, the pink bar turns blue, thus intensifying the reading flow.

Award-winning SaaS Startup Website

Generato helps professional development teams to implement software projects up to 75% faster. The Hamburg-based company has already registered more than 1,000 sign-ups within a few months and recently won the Gründerpreis für Digitale Innovation of the Federal Ministry of Economics.

For Generato, we not only created the website with Webflow, but also integrated various interfaces to intercom and Zapier. In the following the highlights.

1. Intercom & Zapier

As a central business intelligence tool, we have integrated Intercom for Generato. Associated with this was the integration of a chat, onboarding, a documentation and a community forum. This enabled the team to set up an extensive web presence in a short time. Leads collected via the contact form were automatically enriched with Zapier in a database.

2. Hover-Effekt explanations

Generato is a very technical product for professional software developers. To make it easier to understand for visitors who may be less experienced in software development, we have included custom code explanations for individual terms. These are indicated by a hover effect.

3. Custom Pricing

Generato Geschäftsmodell funktioniert mit einem nutzungsbasierten Preismodell. Das bedeutet, dass mit jeder Zeile Code, die das Programm automatisiert schreibt, fällt ein geringer Cent-Betrag an. Nutzer können auch ein Abonnement abschließen, dass eine bestimmte Anzahl an Codezeilen beinhaltet. Für das Pricing haben wir mit Custom Code ein Dropdown-Menü hinzugefügt, dass durch Auswahl eines Paketes automatisch den monatlichen/ jährlichen Preis anpasst.

Revolutionary learning platform used in healthcare

Medinspector is an application by Viandar GmbH, which specializes in a digital medication management system in the healthcare industry. Patients are cared for by an interdisciplinary team of doctors, pharmacists, etc. to ensure high drug therapy safety.
Medinspector's goal is to provide pharmaceutical professionals with an automated, digital training process for Viandar’s application. Team leaders have the option of inviting their staff via email to complete the training online. If they pass, an automation tool immediately sends an individual certificate to the user. In the following the summarized highlights.

1. Automated user management

Due to the integration of the automation tool Integromat, Medinspector's user accounts can be managed dynamically via several API connections. In the CRM application weclapp, for example, a new customer can simply be created and Integromat automatically takes care of the synchronization with the user management system Memberstack.

2. Articulate-integration for interactive learning

Articulate was used to develop the learning application and was integrated on the website with custom code in Webflow . This allows the user to learn the material autodidactically and interactively. The application is made up of various elements, such as learning videos or specific questions.

3. Invitation function for registered team leaders

If you want to prepare your team members for using Viandars software quickly and easily, simply send them an invitation link on the Medinspector website. Integromat is also used here, it processes the invitation and activates a corresponding registration.
