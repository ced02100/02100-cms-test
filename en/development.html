We kickstart your next project with Low-Code

Learn more

Realize high-end Web-Projects with Low-Code.

We work with state-of-the-art technologies to realize your projects in the best possible way. Thanks to Low-Code user interfaces we work faster and more sustainable compared to conventional development. Take a look for yourself:

Our process. Your success.

We have developed a process that ensures the successful implementation of your tailor-made projects. Our focus is on transparent communication and active exchange.

Discovery

Get to know each other and discuss goals.

01-Discovery Call

During a first consultation we will discuss your current and future goals and determine the scope of work, schedule and budget. Using a suitable sample project, we explain how we normally proceed and, if necessary, we arrange a second appointment to plan the scope of your project even more precisely. Finally, we discuss the next steps.

02-Offer

After the Discovery Call, we will send you a summary of the project. This includes: Requirements, schedule and budget. Already here you can give feedback: We create the quote in a Google Docs document, where you can make live comments.
Feedback

03-Contract

As soon as the general conditions for your project are clarified, we formulate the joint service agreement, which is signed digitally (e-sign).

Setup

Your foundation for success.

04-Communication Setup

Communication is key for a successful project. Therefore we would like to be in direct exchange with you at any time. Of course, we are guided by your preferred medium. Our favourite: A shared channel in Slack.

05-Project Plan

Following the same line of thought of the previous point, all project participants should always be up to date. It is therefore important to centralize any planning. Once we have agreed on the communication medium, we will create the project plan, map the tasks in Airtable and invite you and your team to an overview.

Design

Create something that captivates customers.

06-Stylesheet

In order to identify the best possible design for your project, we create as many stylesheets as necessary until you are satisfied. A stylesheet summarizes your corporate identity and includes font and font size, color code, design forms and, if necessary, the logo of your project. We use the information from the previous discussions to create a meaningful design. On average, it takes 3-4 iterations of 2-3 style sheets each until our clients commit themselves. Don't worry: Your stylesheet is only used as an orientation for the creation of the wireframes and adjustments are possible at any time.
3-4 Iterations

07-Wireframe

As soon as you have decided on a favorite stylesheet, we will start creating wireframes. This means that, following the stylesheet and the speeches, we create your project in a design tool like Adobe XD, Sketch or Figma. This design is the basis for the migration to Webflow in a later step. Here we create one wireframe per page/view of your application. As long as you are not satisfied with every page of your application, we will continue to iterate. Due to the previous communication, however, it usually only needs a few iterations here. The average is 4-5 feedback loops per wireframe.
4-5 Iterations

08-Clickdummy

Before we start with the complete migration of your project to Webflow, we create a wireframe of your project in Webflow. With this we want to make sure that you get a feeling for how the site will behave and feel later (keyword: "look and feel"). The background for this is that the design and your actual website usually differ in terms of look and feel (screen size, browser etc.). At this point the heart of your website, the landing page, is usually migrated to Webflow in advance. This way you have a kind of click dummy after a short time.

Webflow

Where the magic happens.

09-Development

Now, the actual development begins. As soon as you are satisfied with your landing page, we start with the creation of the remaining pages. Here you have the possibility to give feedback at any time. You will receive daily updates from us and can check the progress of your Airtable project at any time. We start with the creation of your website for desktop screen sizes. Webflow offers a lot of functionality to easily optimize desktop pages for mobile screen sizes. If required, we also create a completely new page for mobile screen sizes. The feedback loop for this step is the most time-consuming in many projects Layout adjustments as well as feedback on animations are done on several pages and most of the time there are still basic change requests.
Constant Feedack

10-Responsiveness

After you are 100% satisfied with the desktop implementation of your project, we start with the optimization for mobile devices. This step should not be underestimated: 90% of all website calls come from mobile devices. Thanks to Webflow, however, this step is implemented relatively quickly. The feedback here is very similar to the previous step.
Constant Feedback

11-On-Page SEO

Once the desktop and mobile sides are completed, the goal is to ensure you get the reach you deserve. Besides standard optimizations (headings H1-H4, meta tags etc.) this includes: XML sitemaps, OpenGraph, Robots.txt, redirects, BEM class names and dynamic meta data (through Webflow CMS content).

Launch

Final preparations and delivery.

12-Training

Your project is now optimized and ready for release. However, we do not want to hand over a project to you that you do not understand. Therefore, we send you a handover video with Loom as well as dedicated explanations/learning hints for special features of your project.

13-Transfer

Wenn es keine weiteren Fragen gibt, dann transferieren wir Ihr Projekt zu Ihrem Webflow Account und stellen sicher, dass Sie das richtige Webflow Abonnement haben. Es findet eine letzte Qualittässicherung statt und dann ist Ihr Projekt bereit für den Launch.

14-Launch

We block the day of your launch in our diary because: Even though we make sure that everything is implemented correctly through repeated quality assurance sessions, first testers usually still find 1-2 little things.

15-Support

Even after the handover and launch, we would be pleased if we could continue to work on your web presence. If required, we will conclude a maintenance contract, which will give you the security that everything will be fine for future releases. Our slack channel will be maintained in any case!

Die Vorteile von Low-Code. Kurz und knapp.

The advantages of Low-Code summarized: Time savings, quality gains and productivity boosts.

01

Writing code manually is history.

Low-code allows to realize professional web projects - without writing a single line of code. This not only saves time but also increases the quality of the code: Manual programming errors are impossible. Since low code makes manual programming largely obsolete, the complexity of projects is reduced.

02

Move faster. Sustainably.

Realize your projects in a fraction of the time without any loss of quality. Low-code applications have reusable elements that make new projects even easier to implement. However, increased speed does not mean that quality is lost and problems will occur in the future: For example, many low-code applications do not need to be maintained or updated.

03

Save resources and boost productivity.

Automate your internal processes and do not waste time on unnecessary tasks. Let multiple teams work on projects simultaneously and avoid iterative feedback loops between departments. With Low-Code your marketing team can make changes to the software! Development only takes on tasks that cannot be implemented without programming.
